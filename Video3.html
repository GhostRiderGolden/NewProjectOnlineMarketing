Being Tracked?

A good way to check if you are being tracked or not throughout the web or not would have to be seeing the URL for the website you are on. If the website starts with a HTTP, then you know that it is a secure website.

Cookies are files that are on your computer that are able to track where you have been and what pages that you have seen. Most websites show a message about cookies, this is where you most people click on accept cookies without knowing what it means.

What are Cookies?

There are 2 types of cookies, One is called Session Cookies and the other is called Persistent Cookies.

Session cookies are good, they let us move from one page to another without having to relog into the website again. These are good because they do not track you when you log off and sign out of the page.

Persistent Cookies on the other hand, are placed in your computer and allow you to be trackered. For example, as stated in the video. If you visit a shoe store, you might be later bombarded with shoe store ads.

More information

Many people are not afraid if information is being collected about them, until they do not realize that information about their age, name, gender, and address is being collected.

It is impossible to stop being tracked fully, but there are many ways to stop being tracked.

Some ways that help stop being tracked would have to be to clear you history of your browser every time you visit a page, and to remember to clear your cookies. Look at a secure browser when going on the internet such as firefox, which clears cookies for you.
