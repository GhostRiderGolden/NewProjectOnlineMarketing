Being Tracked?

One way to help protect you on the internet would have to be is with passwords. You should always have long passwords that are very difficult to break into.

A good way to check if your password is good enough would have to be going to a specific website online to check how long it would take people to break into your account.

Always remember to have 2-factor authentication as it gives your account more security.

What are Cookies?

Make sure to always check what add-ons you are using, because some add-ons might take more information from you than you know of.

VPNs allow you to stop getting tracked from websites and cookies. Try to avoid free VPNs and to avoid proxies as well. A VPN is a must when going to the web.

For social media, try not to use real names and addresses if people are able to track you. You should not give social media access to your information, such as allowing Snapchat to access your text messages.
