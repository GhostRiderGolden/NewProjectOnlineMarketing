What is Internet Privacy?

Internet privacy is becoming an increasingly big thing now, as in a study it has been shown that 86 percent of Americans are trying to protect their data privacy in many ways than others such as deleting their cookies, encrypting their email addresses, and finally protecting their IP addresses.

The Federal Trade Commission Act 1914 (FTC 1914) was basically an Act that makes it so that it can regulate any unfair commercial practices. This mainly includes complying with privacy policies and personal information.

People expose themselves to the internet through E-mail addresses, banking, passwords, physical addresses, and phone numbers. There are other numerous ways that people expose themselves.

Acts of Data Privacy

Fair Accurate Credit Transactions Act (FACTA 2003) allows financial institutions and creditors to make them written theft prevention programs.

Financial Services Modernization ACT (GLBA 1999) allowed the disclosure of personal information to be collected or held by financial institutions and required many customers notice of this with written information throughout their security program.

Electronic Communications Privacy ACT (ECPA 1986) allowed it so that it protects certain wire, oral and electronic communications, through unauthorized access or permission.

More information

Some things that you can do to protect your personal information would have to be keeping our anti-virus software up to date.Also, another thing would have to be using the most secure internet browser, such as Google chrome and Microsoft edge.

Cookies and web tracking, Cookies are files that are downloaded to your browser by a website that is able to identify your data about from the site itself. However, they do not provide any personal information.

HTTPS allows encryption for information sent between a website and a user's machine. So wherever you go to a website and see a website that has the HTTPs in the beginning of the URL, this is something that you will know that is trustworthy.

Cloud computing is new technology that has come out that brings new issues for data privacy, it allows it so that you never know who has “keys of the kingdom” to view your data.
