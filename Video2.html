More information

There are 2 different types of users, one of which is called the SuperUser and the other is called the normal user.

The normal user will not be able to do anything to your database such as editing, they will only be able to view at your discretion. Whereas, a SuperUser will be able to have full access to the account and will be able to do changes to the system.

A way to track users will be through what is called Device Users, and device users will have a numerical digit numbers and letters in their ID.
